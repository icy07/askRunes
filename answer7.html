The past is represented by Tiwaz in reversed position, which suggests that you have faced challenges in achieving your goals or fulfilling your ambitions. There may have been obstacles, delays, or setbacks that hindered your progress. You may have felt like you were fighting against the tide, struggling to gain traction or make meaningful advancements. This suggests that you might have felt a sense of frustration or disappointment, as if your efforts were not being acknowledged or appreciated.

The present is signified by Othala in reversed position, suggesting that you are experiencing a sense of instability or loss of control over your life. This might manifest as a feeling of insecurity, a lack of support, or the breakdown of established structures or traditions. Perhaps you are struggling to maintain a sense of belonging or find your place in the world. You might be feeling uprooted or disoriented, lacking the foundation you need to thrive. This could be an indication that the environment or circumstances you are currently in do not align with your values or aspirations.

The future is represented by Thurisaz in reversed position, which indicates that there are obstacles or barriers preventing you from moving forward. This may involve internal conflict, suppressed emotions, or a sense of being stuck in a rut. You might be experiencing fear, resistance, or a lack of confidence that hinders your ability to take action or make progress. It is important to address these internal challenges and overcome these obstacles to pave the way for a more positive future.

The overall message of this rune spread suggests that you are in a transitional phase, where you are facing challenges and experiencing a sense of instability. This is not necessarily a negative sign but an opportunity to reassess your priorities and make necessary changes. However, the reversed runes point towards the need to address internal conflicts, overcome obstacles, and gain a stronger sense of purpose to progress towards a more fulfilling path. While becoming an engineer might appeal to you, this spread suggests that the current circumstances may not be conducive to achieving your goals in this area. It might be a good time to explore other possibilities and find a path that aligns with your true needs and aspirations.
