FEHU

Fehu is the first rune of the Elder Futhark, representing wealth, prosperity, and abundance. It symbolizes material and financial gain, as well as personal and spiritual growth. When Fehu appears in a reading, it is a sign that you are entering a period of prosperity and abundance. It may indicate success in your career, a windfall of money, or a sense of financial security. However, it is important to remember that wealth is not just about money, but also about your personal well-being, happiness, and fulfillment. Fehu also urges you to be mindful of how you use your resources and to be generous with your wealth. It encourages you to share your blessings with others and to give back to the community. By doing so, you create a cycle of abundance that benefits everyone involved. In terms of spirituality, Fehu reminds you to pay attention to the things that truly matter in life. It encourages you to focus on your values, beliefs, and spiritual growth, rather than being consumed by material possessions. By cultivating a sense of gratitude and appreciation for what you have, you can attract even more abundance into your life. Overall, Fehu is a positive and auspicious rune that promises the fulfillment of your desires and goals. It encourages you to embrace abundance in all its forms and to live a life of prosperity, generosity, and fulfillment.

Direct reading

Fehu is a rune that represents wealth, abundance, prosperity, and material gain. It is also associated with nourishment and the energy needed to sustain life. When drawn in a reading, Fehu suggests that financial matters and opportunities for growth and success are on the horizon. It can also indicate a need to focus on your own well-being and ensure that you are taking care of yourself in all areas of life. Overall, Fehu is a positive rune that signifies good fortune and the rewards of hard work.

Reversed reading

The reversed reading of the rune Fehu indicates a lack of material wealth or financial stability. It may suggest that there is a loss of abundance or resources, or that there is a greediness or excessive focus on material possessions. It could also signify a need to be cautious with finances and to avoid risky investments or spending habits. In a more positive light, it may symbolize a lesson in the importance of non-material wealth and finding happiness and fulfillment in things other than money and possessions.
